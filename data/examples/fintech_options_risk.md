# Fintech Options Risk Analytics Brief

Source: Lucy portfolio work on Black-Scholes, Method of Lines, crypto analytics, Pandas and Plotly.

Owner: Lucy Nowacki
Deadline: 2026-07-22

Project description: create a fintech analytics brief that explains how option-pricing routines and time-series risk views could support a growth or risk team. Include Python, SQL, dashboard requirements, data dependencies and validation checks. The brief should translate mathematical modelling into stakeholder-ready analytics rather than investment advice.

Risks: financial risk output could be misused as investment advice, market data quality may be weak, and model assumptions should be reviewed. Priority is high because the project will be used to demonstrate analytics credibility for fintech and startup roles.
