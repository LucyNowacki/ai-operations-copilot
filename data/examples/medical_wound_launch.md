# Medical AI Wound Diagnostics Launch Brief

Source: Lucy portfolio themes around wound diagnostics, on-the-go healthcare, CancerDetector and AzureCancerDetector.

Owner: Lucy Nowacki
Due: 2026-07-15

Meeting notes: prepare an AI project launch brief for a mobile wound-diagnostics assistant. The system should help clinicians triage wound images, prioritise urgent cases and document treatment questions. It must not make autonomous clinical decisions. The demo should show image-model evaluation, patient-safety review, PII awareness and Azure deployment handoff. The business audience wants to understand how the project reduces triage delay while keeping clinicians in control.

Known concerns: clinical safety, patient data, source checking, hallucination risk and whether the model should provide treatment suggestions. We need a human approval gate before any medical output is finalised.
