## How AI Operations Copilot Extends That Profile

AI Operations Copilot keeps the same applied-AI and mathematical direction, but moves it closer to employer-facing production workflows:

- From semantic recommendation to operational action recommendation.
- From notebooks to a tested Python package with a CLI.
- From CSV experiments to repeatable CSV-to-SQLite ingestion.
- From deployable ML apps to a workflow that can connect to n8n, Slack, Teams, CRM tools or internal APIs.
- From financial modelling to ARR-at-risk reporting and fintech operations triage.
- From AI-agent workflow ideas to a concrete human-in-the-loop approval system.
